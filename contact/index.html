Join Us

Graduates

We welcome graduate students under the UIC academic units of Engineering, chemistry, biochemistry, biology, physics, or biophysics to visit and join our lab. For additional information about ongoing research projects, please send inquiry to Shafigh Mehraeen (tranzabi@uic.edu).

Undergraduates

Undergraduate students who are interested in doing undergraduate research in our lab may apply to join through the Chancellor''s Undergraduate Research Awards. We seek highly motivated students who make a commitment to a research project, ideally during an academic year or a summer. To apply, please contact Shafigh Mehraeen (tranzabi@uic.edu) with CV and a brief description of research interests.
